FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from students and parents.

What are your working hours?

FA is open Monday to Saturday, 9:00am to 6:00pm. We are closed on Sundays. You can also reach us via WhatsApp at any time and we will respond during working hours.

Can I visit the institute before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage you to visit us at S-2, Marutham Enclave, Hosur. Our counsellors will give you a tour of the facilities, including our labs and library, and help you choose the right program.

Is online counselling available?

Yes. You can schedule an online counselling session via phone or video call. Call us at +91 8098-301-702 or send a WhatsApp message and we will arrange a convenient time.

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during working days. For urgent queries, please call us directly or message us on WhatsApp for the fastest response.

Can I order study materials for home delivery?

Yes! FA's study materials can be ordered for delivery across Hosur and surrounding areas. Contact us with your program details and we will arrange delivery. You can also collect in person from our centre.

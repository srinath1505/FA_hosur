Our Story

The History of Finance Academy

F.A. – the Finance Academy was founded with a singular mission: to make world-class finance education accessible to every student in and around Hosur, Tamil Nadu. What began as a small coaching centre has grown into a fully equipped institution with dedicated labs, a library, and a publishing arm.

Today, FA stands as Hosur's most trusted finance coaching institute — with a track record of producing rank holders, state toppers and successful CA professionals year after year.

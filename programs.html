FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our programs? We have answers.

What is the eligibility for CA Foundation?

Students who have passed Class 12 (10+2) from any recognized board are eligible to register for the CA Foundation course. Registration must be done at least 4 months before the exam. FA helps you with the full registration process.

Which learning mode is best for a working professional?

We recommend Online Live or Recorded Video modes for working professionals. These allow you to study at your convenience without disrupting your work schedule. Our custom coaching plans are also a popular choice.

Does FA provide study material?

Yes! FA has its own publishing house that produces comprehensive, exam-focused study material for all papers. This is included in your program fee and is regularly updated to reflect the latest syllabus.

Are there mock tests and practice exams?

Absolutely. FA maintains a question bank of 10,000+ questions and conducts regular mock tests, topic tests and full-length exam simulations. These are available to all enrolled students across all programs.

How do I enroll in a program?

You can visit us at Marutham Enclave, Hosur, call us at +91 8098-301-702, or fill out the contact form on our website. Our counsellors will guide you to the right program and learning mode based on your goals and schedule.

How We Teach

9 Ways to Learn at FA

Education that fits your life — not the other way around. Choose from 9 flexible learning modes designed for every schedule and learning style.

Regular Classroom

Traditional face-to-face instruction in our state-of-the-art Hosur campus. Structured schedules, peer learning, and direct tutor access.

Online Live

Live interactive sessions streamed to your device. Attend from anywhere with the same real-time engagement as a classroom.

Recorded Videos

High-quality recorded lectures available on demand. Watch, rewind and master topics at your own pace, anytime.

One-on-One

Exclusive personalised sessions with a dedicated tutor. Tailored entirely to your pace, goals and areas of focus.

Crash Course

Intensive revision programs designed for exam-readiness. Cover the full syllabus efficiently in a condensed, high-impact format.

Hybrid Mode

The best of classroom and online learning combined. Attend in-person when you can; switch to online when you need flexibility.

Home Tuition

FA tutors come to you. Ideal for students who prefer learning in their own environment with maximum comfort and focus.

Self-Study Kit

FA's comprehensive study kit — books, question banks and guides — delivered to your door for self-paced independent study.

Group Coaching

Small-group coaching for collaborative learning. Study with a curated peer group for shared motivation and competitive progress.
